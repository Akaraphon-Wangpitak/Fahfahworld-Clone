FAHFAHS

— Illustrator / TH —


Our products are designed by FAHFAHS
a Thai illustrator.

FAHFAHS, “I started drawing when I was a child and now I usually have a pen and paper with me to sit and draw whenever I have free time. As I scribbled on the paper, I gradually developed a figure I drew often into a cartoon character named FAHFAHS. I think what makes my work distinct is the neatly striped lines of hair, the face of the character, and the coloring. I just started painting with watercolors about three years ago and personally, I find it charming, warm, and sweeter than other techniques that I’ve used.”
